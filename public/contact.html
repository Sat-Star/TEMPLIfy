Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our templates and services.

What is your average response time for support inquiries?

Our support team typically responds to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday through Friday). For urgent matters, we prioritize responses and aim to reply within 4-6 hours.

Do you offer custom template development services?

Yes, we provide custom template development services for clients with specific requirements. Our design team works closely with you to create bespoke templates that align with your brand identity and business objectives.

What is your refund policy for template purchases?

Due to the digital nature of our templates and the comprehensive previews we provide, we maintain a no-refund policy. However, we offer detailed demos, documentation, and previews to ensure you can evaluate our templates thoroughly before purchasing.

Can I modify and resell the templates after purchase?

Our standard license allows for unlimited modifications to the templates for your own use or for client projects. However, reselling the templates as-is or in significantly modified form requires an extended license. Please review our licensing terms for specific details.
